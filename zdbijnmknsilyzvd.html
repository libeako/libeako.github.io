Behringer UMC22 U-Phoria

this is a cheap but quality "audio interface"

it contains both an input and an output DAC+amp

thus it can function as a headphone amplifier too, as such it is neutral sounding but somewhat metalic, but it still is not difficult to listen to for long time

FiiO E10K Olympus 2

cheap, small headphone amplifier

sound

clean, somewhat smoothed; no harshness, no sibilance, no metalic timbre

this makes it easy to listen to for long time

as if it was elevated at around 160 Herz

stage is smaller than with Behringer UMC22

Beyerdynamic A 20

neutral, non-offensive, never harsh, maybe a little more tightness would be better

could drive my Sennheiser Game One with acceptable sound quality; this is notable because that headphone usually sounds unpleasantly wet

Topping L30

is warm all the time, even on smallest gain, even when no sound is played

sound

lean

thin, but still wet

my Sennheiser 6xx sounded awesome on it

my Superlux 672 did not show a big quality improvement by switching from cheap Behringer UMC22 to this amp

Sabaj A10h

similar to Topping L30, but somewhat more blant and less neutral, feels less correct

Topping DX3 Pro+

clean, sweet, warm, too blunt

pushes the music in my face too much, hence spatial properties are bad
