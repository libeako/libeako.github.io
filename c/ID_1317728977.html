libeakotopicsmath, information technologyotherhardwaremicrophonereview of Superlux TM58

this is similar to the legendary Shur SM58, but 3-4 times cheaper

the voice style is good : natural, clear, detailed

the bad is the handling noise being high and the sensitivity being low

sensitivity

i use a Behringer UMC22 amplifier which i think is quite a good one but still its noise creeps into the recording because i have to set the gain to 85-90% to avoid this noise i have to maximize the (signal / noise) value by putting the microphone almost into my mouth and speaking into it from the very front of it this of course aplifies the wind noises and mouth noises but i found that the microphone is good in resisting those, even with this close usage it is not worth than my Sandberg USB Mic Kit was with a condenser microphone from further

handling noise

it is so laud that even if i am not tapping on it, just holding it still in my hand - the noise generated is visible in the waveform compared to having the microphone lie still on a pile of clothes on the desk

wind noise

very good the situation is good even without additional wind filtering but i put on it a very cheap foam cap that significantly reduces the winds and is surely worth to get and use it in my opinion with it the plosives are very silent the biggest problem for me was the breezing wind, but even that is quite good

my recommendation : use a foam cap windscreen, speak into it from very close and from the very front and do not hand-hold
