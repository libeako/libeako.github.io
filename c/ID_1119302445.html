in software coding abstract types representing algebras are analogous to abstract algebras in math abstract types are often in the form of type interfaces

in programming languages like haskell abstract algebras are very popular a popular method of encoding them is type interfaces ["type classes"] i also will use that them here in my illustrations [with my haskell-like syntax]
