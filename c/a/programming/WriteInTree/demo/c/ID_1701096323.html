Demonstration and Tutorial of WriteInTree → Basic workflow →

You write your book's contents in mindmap format first, using the also free software "Freeplane". Writing your book in this software will give you the biggest possible comfort during writing - you will not have to deal with technical details like formatting at this stage. Freeplane will make you able to structure and restructure your content with the biggest possible ease, so you can unleash your full authoring talent.

When the structure and content of your book is ready then you have 2 tasks left:

Review your work in its entirity to improve its quality.

Specify format how you want your book look like when rendered into html website. You do this by attaching special nodes to your mindmap. What special nodes and how they will control the translation into website is defined by the write-in-tree technology.

Often it is best to do these 2 taks at the same time.
