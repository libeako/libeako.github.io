Paragraph

Each leaf text node represents a paragraph.

Several paragraphs can be united into 1 single by putting them under a "# paragraph" node. This may be necessary if you want to make only a portion of a paragraph a link.

You can create a space with a "# space" node. It is an inline element.

Sections

Paragraphs can be grouped into a section - just put them under a plain text node, whose content will be the title of the section.

Section may contain other section. The depth of the nesting is not limited technically, but it is a bad practise to go further than using a sub-section of a section. If you find yourself in such situation, then consider sub-pages instead.

Sub-pages

Create a "# linked-contents" node, whose children will be the sub-pages. The text content of the immediate child nodes will be the titles of the subpages and also the text of the links to the subpages.
