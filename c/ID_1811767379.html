politicians against freedom of speech like to hide their attacks on the freedom of speech in the technicalities of speech by constraining the usage of technologies for speech hence the constitution should defend against these kind of attack explicitly too

terms :

"technic" :

the technical implementation of speech

"network provider" :

operates the technical side of the internet

transmits and controls the data traffic over its network

to customers : provides ability to connect to the network

"content distributor" :

makes material [normally created by someone else] available, discoverable, searchable

the technic is practically the internet but it must be defined generally it also includes press, phone, ...

who prepares a serious crime : makes the effort necessary to walk around the technical constrains by the state on speech hence state constrains are working rather over law abiding people, but over them is is harmful overall

till 2010 there was not technical limitation on the internet in most western countries, and that did not cause significant problem

content and source filtering

the constitution must prevent the state from filtering certain content or source [internet address]

on one side : nobody wants that everything was available on the internet [including recipes for chemical weapons for example], but on the other side : experience shows that constrains tend to be used by the ruler tyrannically which is a much bigger danger hence it is much safer to allow everything

technic providers must not be responsible for the actions of their customers

treating content distributors responsible for the content that they distribute [but did not create] is so sensible as treating bus drivers responsible for transporting criminals to the crime scene

such laws are tools in the rulers' hands that can be used [beside wealth corruption] to threaten content distributors to cooperate in tyrannical attempts [for example surveillance]

net neutrality - the state should not force it

though i am against discrimination : i think : discrimination is less danger then the ability of the state to intervene

the law prohibiting discrimination can be used as a weapon against the network providers to threaten them into cooperation in for example content filtering and surveillance [beside wealth corruption]

decades of allowing discrimination has already proved that it works okay in practice
