state of emergency is not good reason to suspend pillars of democracy

tyrants and wanna be tyrants like to suspend the pillars of democracy state of emergency is an excuse for that but it is an illegitimate excuse in the sense that there is actually no reason to believe that the democratic pillars should be weaker in an emergency

for example here are some pillars of democracy, and hopefully you can see about them one by one that they should not be suspended at all during any emergency :

freedom of speech

freedom of assembly and protest

froodom of organizing groups

the power of the parliament over the government

not allowing state propaganda

the freedom of support for politics from abroad

even contrary : in an emergency the decisions of the political elite are more important, hence their democratic control is more important
