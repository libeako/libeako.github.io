speaker

very good, like the JVC HA-RX330

good, much better by a category than JVC HA-RX330 and Sennheiser HD 206, similar to Superlux HD 681 EVO

better than JVC HA-RX330 and Sennheiser HD 206 because detail is much better but much worse than Superlux HD 681 EVO i very often find a song ehh and when i re-listen with the Superlux then i inderstand what the song is about compared to the Superlux : some higher frequency sounds are missing this is also a big issue in movies and games : small arms fire sounds so bad i first almost loughed myself loudly, missile passing me sounds nothing, metal objects scraping each other sounds almost inaudible

bass

this speaker is closed back and has 50 mm drivers, it is also biased towards lows but bass is still not good it has some wierd vibration that makes it wild and destroys it - it is very annoying otherwise nice deep sounds sound awful but sometimes it comes out good for example when i watch Jurassic park dinosaurs roar or grizzles fight with each other then i feel like i am there and these animals will crush me and i can do nothing but sometimes it is really bad for example tank engine sounds bad, deep cover in music sounds bad

overall : the speaker quality is not horrible it is definitely better than JVC HA-RX330 and Sennheiser HD 206 in almost all aspects but it is bad compared to the Superlux HD 681 EVO the nice immersion and warm pleasant tone [like with the JVC HA-RX330] compensates somewhat

cord

only 2 meters long

braided

it is touching my neck and t-shirt, because it comes out from the headset in a wrong direction - this is annoying but i suspect that one can get used to it

overall : this is not a good headset, has multiple serious problems, but it is not horrible if you can get it with a discount then it may be worth the price
