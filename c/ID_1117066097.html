for any algebraic variety V :

the free V, by definition, is a data structure that for any algebra A of variety V : is able to represent any algorithm consisting of only the values and methods of A and is not able to represent anything else

free V is itself an algebra of variety V

some people phrase this fact as "the free V gives as a V for free" some people may thing that this is where the word "free" comes from but that is false "free" comes from math, more precisely from the fact that free v can not represent anything else than algorithms in an algebra of V
