abstraction scares many coders, but actually it should attract you

because

abstraction is a big friend of a coder because it help much in code-reuse, separating concerns, which are fundamental goals in coding

the difficulty in abstraction is at the beginning, to understand it once you understand it : it is simple, easy to remember and powerful in usage since when you will understand it : you will use it in your daily work and it will sink in you, quickly you will feel like you always knew it, from birth it will become natural for you and you will easily generalize it, apply it in new fields, extend it step-by-step to the degree that you may have felt impossible earlier

once you do this for one abstraction : feeling how much easier it is than you felt before and how much you gained by it : you will be eager to learn the next one, and so on
