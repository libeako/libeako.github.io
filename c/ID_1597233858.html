i had trouble understanding "defunctionalization" the reason was i expected it to be some complex compiler technique but in reality it is something very basic : the reification of functions, that is : representing functions by plain data structures

the opposite of defunctionalization is functionalization of plain data, like church encoding

Defunctionalization at Work by Olivier Danvy and Lasse Nielsen investigates how these 2 functions may be considered as mathematical inverses
