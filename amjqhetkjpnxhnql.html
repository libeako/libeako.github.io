representation of conjunction and disjunction

rep (S1 and S2) := S1 * S2

rep (S1 or S2) := S1 + S2

this is very simple and intuitive

it is the product type that has a value exactly when both of its components types have values

similarly for disjunction

these 2 type constructions [product, sum] are duals of each other and the logical construct types that they induce are duals of each other too
