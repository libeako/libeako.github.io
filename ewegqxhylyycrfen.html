a 'Riemann sample' on a division is a set of points, one from each section of the division

a 'Riemann sum' is

sum {forall section of the division. the function's output at the sample of the section * the length of the section}

the 'Riemann integral' exists and equals to l iff

forall dy > 0. exists dx > 0. forall division

dx >= the max section length of the division

=>

dy >= distance (the Riemann sum over that division) l
