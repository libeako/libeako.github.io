theorem:

in every infinite subsequence

exists a subsequence which is monotone increasing

exists a subsequence which is monotone decreasing

proof

let 'peak point' mean the property of a position that the element there is greater or equal to all the later elements

suppose the amount if peak points is infinite

then they form a monotone increasing sub-sequence

else

we can always find a greater element later; this proves that a monotone increasing sequence exists

the existence of a monotone decreasing sequence is similarly provable

usual names fot this theorem include "peak point lemma", "spanish hotel theorem"
