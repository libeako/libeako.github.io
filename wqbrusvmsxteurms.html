link to parametricity

the parametricity relation corresponding to functors is

(related (fx1 : F X1) (fx2 : F X2) by (f : X1 -> X2)) = (map f fx1 = fx2)

i can not prove it, but it is intuitive: when we thinking about functors as containers: 2 containers are equivalent iff their elements are equivalent
