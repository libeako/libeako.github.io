politicians against freedom of speech like to hide their attacks on the freedom of speech in the technicalities of speech; by constraining the usage of technologies for speech; hence the constitution should defend against these kind of attacks explicitly too

terms :

"technic" :

the technical implementation of speech

"network provider" :

operates the technical side of the internet

transmits and controls the data traffic over its network

to customers : provides ability to connect to the network

"content distributor" :

makes material [normally created by someone else] available, discoverable, searchable

the technic is practically the internet; but it must be defined generally; it also includes press, phone, ...

serious criminals dodge the constrains

who prepares a serious crime : makes the effort necessary to walk around the technical constrains by the state on speech; hence state constrains are working rather over law abiding people; but over them it is harmful overall

total freedom already proved

till 2010 there was no technical limitation on the internet in most western countries, and that did not cause significant problem

content and source filtering

the constitution must prevent the state from filtering certain content or source [internet address]

on one side : nobody wants that everything was available on the internet [including recipes for chemical weapons for example], but on the other side : experience shows that constrains tend to be used by the ruler tyrannically; which is a much bigger danger; hence it is much safer to allow everything

the responsibility of technic providers

technic providers must not be responsible for the actions of their customers

treating content distributors responsible for the content that they distribute [but did not create] is so sensible as treating bus drivers responsible for transporting criminals to the crime scene

such laws are tools in the rulers' hands that can be used [beside wealth corruption] to threaten content distributors to cooperate in tyrannical attempts [for example surveillance]

individual access

the state must not constrain the access of the technic [for example the internet] by individuals

the danger of this is unfortunately serious

in France before 2015 was a law created to ban people from using the internet just because they illegally downloaded muic

in USA "sex offenders" can be banned from using the internet for multiple years and to get this badge it is enough to have sex with someone under 14

the banning from internet is mainly not punishment but prevention of repeating the prohibited act; but it is too strict against the not dangerous criminals [the internet is the main place of self-realization, work, education, ... - the most important aspects of our lives] and too ineffective against the dangerous criminals

the power to ban access to the internet carries the danger of tyranny

it can disable influencers

people without internet access can not get informed, spread its messages, defend its reputation in society against false accusations

net neutrality

the state should not force it

though i am against discrimination : i think : discrimination is less danger than the ability of the state to intervene

the law prohibiting discrimination can be used as a weapon against the network providers to threaten them into cooperation in for example content filtering and surveillance [beside wealth corruption]

decades of allowing discrimination has already proved that it works okay in practice

in some cases discrimination is useful for the society

some clients buying extra bandwidth is does not hurt other clients and helps in the development of the infrastructure

privileging important low consuming usages [for example wikipedia] over not important high consuming usages [game streaming] may be good
