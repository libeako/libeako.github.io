one of the many if not the most legendary audiophile headphone

the skeleton seems to be strong, gives confidence

more than with the Sennheiser 5 types

comfort is good

mass is low

advantage over Sennheiser 5 types is that those contain a small bumb in the inside of the cups

the headband padding is not so comfy as with the cheaper Sennheiser 5** headphones, i did feel hotspot on my head.

overall

good headphone but not great, not up to its fame

i still enjoy my Superlux 672 much more

the Superlux has much better separation

the Superlux is more melodic, musical, easier to hear the artistic intent, the Sennheiser 650 is boring, comperatively

the Superlux is much more clear, the Sennheiser is very slightly sibilant, textured, comperatively, which may contribute to the false sense of resolution

i do not see a big jump in quality from Sennheiser 5** headphones, not even in the sound

600

my first surprise was that the sound is somewhat soar, but this feeling disappeared later

stage is said to be very small; i was afraid ot this but it does not bother me, it is even pleasant; the stage of the 650 did bother me

frequency response: very neutral

timbre is too dry, a texture added by the headphone

like if i was listening to loud-speakers

dynamism is too low - makes the sound feel flat

separation is good

easy to listen to for long time

the sound for me is muddy and not just a little but much muddy; so much that i highly recommend to not buy this headphone; this is independent of its frequency response; rather: learn to equalize and buy a Superlux 672 [with better earpads] instead; the 672 is more clean and dynamic and even more comfy; the Sennheiser HD 6** headphones are over-hyped and over-priced, forget them, unless you very much want to avoid both equalizing

650

sounds similar to hd 600, unsurprisingly

it is laid back, but less so than Sennheiser 598 and Game One are

has some punch, unlike the Sennheiser 5 types, but this punch is not a pleasant one for me

has some real base, more than the Sennheiser 5 types

stage is even across the space dimensions but it is small, just at my head surface

has some old style, as if i went back into the 80s, similar to the sound of cheap Koss headphones
