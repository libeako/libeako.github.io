an "ordered" magma is a magma with an ordering such that the operation is "monotone" in it

"monotonity" of operation o with relation r means by definition that

forall x y s

r x y =>

and

r (o x s) (o y s)

r (o s x) (o s y)

an other name of this property is that the operation and the order are "compatible"

more sophisticated algebras [subtypes of magma] also have ordered versions, like ordered monoid
